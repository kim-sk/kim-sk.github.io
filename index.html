Seung Kyung Kim

I received my Ph.D. in 2015 from the Linguistics Department at Stanford University. The title of my dissertation is Speech, variation, and meaning: The effect of emotional prosody on word recognition. I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire Parole et Langage, Aix Marseille Université in France working on the SIRL research project (Social Interaction in the Representation of Language). Currently, I am teaching in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Utah.
