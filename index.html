Seung Kyung Kim

I received my Ph.D. in September 2015 from the Linguistics Department at Stanford University. The title of my dissertation is Speech, variation, and meaning: The effect of emotional prosody on word recognition. In November 2015, I began as a postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire Parole et Langage in France working on the SIRL research project (Social Interaction in the Representation of Language).
